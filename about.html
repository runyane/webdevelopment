Hello and welcome. I’m El, an educator and aspiring instructional designer. I discovered an interest in digital creation early in life. As a young adult, I used to design basic websites on GeoCities. I find the ability to render documents digitally fascinating.

During my time at The University of Memphis, I enjoyed document design. Content creation has become more than just a hobby. However, it was the MAT licensure program that inspired me to learn more about instructional design.

As a professional, I love the process of creating content and designing documents. My understanding of document design helps me regularly improve my teaching practice.

When not trying to design the perfect document, I am a moderate crafter. I make a range of small projects from quilts to paintings. My philosophy is to make education practical and enjoyable. Ultimately, I want to develop my skills to support my students better. I look forward to getting to know everyone.
